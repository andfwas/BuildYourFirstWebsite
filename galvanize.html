FUTURE
Excited Hyped Releived
code addiction love 2 learn long time coming

I learned basic html as a freshman in college from a friend and built a few personal sites that were ugly but I enjoyed the process of building them.

Fast forward 18 years to 2013... I saw the Codecademy creators on tv explaining that anyone can learn to code and even kids are learning it these days. Kids are even building incredible applications! Attracted by the free aspect of Codecademy, I took the html and css courses and was surprised that I actually remembered html, and css seemed to come easily.

The next suggested course was Javascript which I tried and became quickly confused and discouraged. I decided to quit at that point.

Fast forward another 3.5 years... I revisited Codecademy and was surprised to learn that Javascript was no longer "too hard!"

At that point I developed a love and dare I call it an addiction to object oriented programming languages in general. I quickly devoured every available free online course I could get my hands on.

At this point I still considered my obsession a hobby. It wasn't until I quit my most recent job that I was forced to consider web development as a career option. My previous job (and most jobs I have ever had) was intense work for very little pay. After my yearly review, they gave me a very nice title and all the responsibility that went with it without appropriate compensation. It was then that I realized I wasn't actualizing my potential. I also realized I would have to apply myself to learning some new and more specific skills in order to actualize my potential. I have always loved computers and I have been building my own PCs for myself, as well as for my family and friends for over 10 years. I thought, "I'm a frickin computer guy! Let's do something with computers!!"

I have had people tell me that I am "brave" for embarking on a career change at my age. My answer to that is "I think the bravery was in trying to make the previous methods work for SO LONG..." Which is why I am RELIEVED to finally be here!
